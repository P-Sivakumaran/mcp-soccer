# Scouting Note Guidelines

- Voice: concise, professional, evidence-led; avoid hyperbole.
- Context: state season_used and minutes to signal sample confidence.
- Strengths: 2–3 bullet points tied to top percentiles or standout raw stats.
- Risks: 1–2 bullet points tied to weak percentiles or small samples.
- Fit: briefly link metrics to role/style (e.g., progression for possession style).
- Trajectory: mention improving/stable/declining if multi-season signals exist.

Example template:
- Why shortlisted: Role fit for '<role>' with strong <top traits>. Minutes=<minutes> in <season>.
- Strengths: <stat> (<pct>th pct), <stat> (<pct>th pct)
- Risks: <stat> (<pct>th pct), <caveat>
